Homemade Ceramics

Alice is inspired by the idea that functional items can be beautiful, she intends for her ceramics to be used everyday and not just to be a decorative object.

Her creations are also inspired by the love of hosting and cooking. wanting to create ceramics which bring character to serving food and spending time with friends and family.
